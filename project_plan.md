# PROITKEYS — Industry-Oriented IT Training & Career Development Platform

## 1. Project Description
PROITKEYS is a modern online training and career development platform based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. We offer practical, career-focused training in Cloud Computing, AI, Data Science, Cybersecurity, Software Testing, SAP, and Business Intelligence. Our mission is to transform learners into confident, job-ready professionals through industry exposure, expert mentorship, and placement assistance.
